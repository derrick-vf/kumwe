Premium Agricultural Exports


We specialize in exporting high-quality fresh produce, including avocados, chilies, and habaneros, sourced directly from Rwanda's best farms.

  • Fresh Hass Avocados


    Known for their creamy texture and rich flavor, Hass avocados are a favorite worldwide. They have a dark, pebbly skin and a high oil content, making them perfect for guacamole, salads, and spreads.

    Farm Period: Typically takes 12-18 months from planting to first harvest. Trees reach full production in 5-7 years.

    Nutritional Benefits: High in healthy monounsaturated fats, fiber, and essential vitamins like Vitamin E and potassium.

  • Premium Red Chillies


    Vibrant red chilies with a bold, spicy flavor. Ideal for adding heat and color to dishes, sauces, and marinades.

    Hotness Level: Medium to high heat, ranging from 30,000 to 50,000 Scoville Heat Units (SHU).

    Farm Period: Harvested approximately 90-120 days after planting.

    Uses: Commonly used in hot sauces, dried spice blends, and fresh culinary applications.

  • Red Habanero Peppers


    Small, lantern-shaped peppers with a fruity and floral flavor, followed by intense heat.

    Hotness Level: Extremely hot, ranging from 100,000 to 350,000 Scoville Heat Units (SHU).

    Farm Period: Ready for harvest 90-100 days after planting.

    Uses: Perfect for hot sauces, salsas, and adding a fiery kick to dishes.

  • Fresh Fuerte Avocados


    Smooth, thin skin with a buttery texture and slightly nutty flavor. Fuerte avocados are larger than Hass and have a lighter green skin.

    Farm Period: Takes 12-18 months from planting to first harvest, with full production in 5-7 years.

    Nutritional Benefits: Rich in healthy fats, fiber, and vitamins like Vitamin C and folate.

  • Pineapple MD2 Variety


    Known as the "Golden Pineapple," MD2 is sweet, low in acidity, and has a golden-yellow flesh. Itâ€™s one of the most popular pineapple varieties globally.

    Farm Period: Takes about 18-24 months from planting to harvest.

    Nutritional Benefits: High in Vitamin C, manganese, and bromelain, an enzyme with anti-inflammatory properties.

  • Yellow Habanero Peppers


    Bright yellow peppers with a fruity and slightly citrusy flavor, followed by intense heat.

    Hotness Level: Extremely hot, ranging from 100,000 to 350,000 Scoville Heat Units (SHU).

    Farm Period: Ready for harvest 90-100 days after planting.

    Uses: Ideal for hot sauces, pickling, and adding heat to dishes.

  • French Beans


    Tender, slender green beans with a crisp texture and mild flavor. Also known as haricot verts.

    Farm Period: Harvested 50-60 days after planting.

    Nutritional Benefits: High in fiber, Vitamin C, and folate. Low in calories and a great addition to healthy diets.

  • Passion Fruits


    A tropical fruit with a hard, purple or yellow outer shell and juicy, seed-filled pulp. Known for its sweet-tart flavor and aromatic scent.

    Farm Period: Takes about 8-12 months from planting to first harvest.

    Nutritional Benefits: Rich in antioxidants, Vitamin A, and dietary fiber. Supports immune health and digestion.

  • Green Birdeye Chilli


    Small, green chilies with a sharp, intense heat and slightly fruity flavor.

    Hotness Level: Very hot, ranging from 50,000 to 100,000 Scoville Heat Units (SHU).

    Farm Period: Harvested approximately 80-90 days after planting.

    Uses: Commonly used in Asian cuisine, sauces, and as a fresh garnish for added heat.
