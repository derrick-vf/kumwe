Who We Are

Connecting Rwanda's Finest Produce to the World

Kumwe Green Farms Ltd is a horticulture export company committed to delivering high-quality fresh produce to global markets. We specialize in farming, trading, processing, and logistics to ensure a steady supply of premium horticultural products. We collaborate with exporters, farmers, and suppliers to uphold quality and consistency.

Our key markets include the United Kingdom and Europe for vegetables like green chili and hot pepper and the Middle East and Asia for Hass and Fuerte avocados. By leveraging cutting-edge solutions, global market expertise, and strategic partnerships, we aim to establish a strong international presence.
