Our Team

Fred Rwigamba

Founder and CEO

With over a decade of experience in finance and operations, Fred Rwigamba is a visionary leader dedicated to transforming the agricultural sector. As the Founder and CEO of Kumwe Green Farm Ltd, he is passionate about empowering farmers, driving business and market development, and pioneering new market and product launches.

His strategic approach to sustainable growth has positioned Kumwe Green Farm as a globally competitive enterprise. Fred excels in optimizing financial and operational processes, fostering innovation, and creating impactful solutions that enhance productivity and profitability in agriculture.

Stephen Rutikanga

CFO and Head of Operations

As CFO and Head of Operations at Kumwe Green Farms Ltd, he drives financial strategy and operational efficiency to support the company’s export growth. He oversees financial reporting, export finance, risk management, and regulatory compliance while optimizing working capital and providing strategic insights to leadership.

Beyond finance, he manages end-to-end operations, ensuring seamless integration across farming, trading, processing, and logistics. By streamlining supply chain processes and maintaining quality, he enhances efficiency and strengthens the company’s presence in key markets across the UK, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.

Allen Kayesu

Data Analyst

Allen Kayesu is pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics at the University of New York, Abu Dhabi. At Kumwe, she applies data-driven insights to optimize horticulture and export operations.

By analyzing supply chain data and identifying key trends, she enhances productivity, efficiency, and strategic decision-making across the organization. Her work ensures seamless operations and maximized yields through the power of analytics.

Shallon Kobusinge

IT Infrastructure Engineer

Shallon Kobusinge is pursuing a Computer Science degree with a minor in Information and Data Science at the California Institute of Technology.

At Kumwe Green Farms, she designs, implements, and maintains the company’s IT infrastructure, ensuring reliable, secure, and high-performance systems that support business operations.
